Gym Resources – On Campus and Nearby

Finding the right workout environment makes it easier to stay consistent. USC students can choose between on-campus gyms included with tuition or specialized gyms nearby.

On-Campus Facilities

Included with Tuition

Strom Thurmond Wellness and Fitness Center

Hours: Mon-Fri 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM; Sat-Sun 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Highlights: Weight room, cardio machines, indoor track, swimming pool, rock-climbing wall, group classes, basketball courts

Why Choose Strom: Modern, spacious, and filled with options for all fitness levels

Considerations: Often crowded in the evenings

Included with Tuition

Blatt Physical Education Center

Hours: Mon-Fri: 6–8:30 AM, 11:30 AM–1:30 PM, 4:30–7:00 PM; Sat-Sun 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Highlights: Free weights, cardio machines, basketball & volleyball courts, indoor track

Why Choose Blatt: Quieter and less crowded, great for quick workouts

Considerations: Smaller equipment selection compared to Strom

Nutrition and Food Options

Fitness isn’t just about working out — it’s about what you eat, too. Good nutrition fuels your workouts and helps you recover. USC provides dining options on campus, but smart choices make the difference.

🍴 On-Campus Dining Tips

Make the most of your meal plan with these smart choices

  • Russell House Dining Hall
    Build balanced meals with lean proteins, whole grains, and vegetables
  • Healthy Grab-and-Go
    Choose wraps, yogurt, and fruit over chips and soda
  • Grubhub Ordering
    Pre-order healthy meals to avoid last-minute unhealthy choices

🛒 Grocery Shopping on a Budget

Local stores like Aldi, Walmart, and Trader Joe’s offer affordable options. With $30/week, you can shop for:

Proteins

Chicken Eggs Tuna Beans

Carbs

Rice Oats Whole wheat bread Sweet potatoes

Fruits & Veggies

Bananas Spinach Apples Frozen veggies

💲 Sample Weekly Meal Plan

Affordable, nutritious meals to fuel your fitness journey

  • Breakfast — Oatmeal with banana and peanut butter
  • Lunch — Brown rice, beans, and grilled chicken
  • Snack — Greek yogurt with fruit
  • Dinner — Whole wheat pasta with spinach and ground turkey
